About the Maldives

The Maldives, an archipelago of 1,192 coral islands, offers a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, luxurious resorts, and vibrant marine life. A prime destination for relaxation and adventure, it boasts unspoiled beaches, pristine reefs, and spectacular underwater experiences.

History of the Maldives

The Maldives has a rich history, dating back over 2,000 years. It was originally settled by fishermen from the Indian subcontinent and later influenced by traders and explorers. The islands were known for their maritime culture and have been a key point in the Indian Ocean trade routes.

Maldivian Culture

The Maldives has a vibrant culture blending South Asian, Arab, and African influences. Known for its traditional music, dance, and cuisine, the Maldivian way of life revolves around community, the ocean, and hospitality. The people of the Maldives are warm and welcoming, making it a delightful destination for tourists.
