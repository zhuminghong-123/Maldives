Luxury Resorts in the Maldives

The Maldives is home to some of the most luxurious resorts in the world, offering private villas, overwater bungalows, and breathtaking views of the turquoise waters. These resorts cater to every need, from private beaches to world-class dining and spa services.

Best Resorts to Visit

  • One&Only Reethi Rah: Known for its unparalleled luxury and exquisite overwater villas.
  • Soneva Jani: An eco-friendly resort offering private island experiences and fine dining.
  • Vakkaru Maldives: Ideal for nature lovers, with stunning coral reefs and a focus on sustainability.
